How it works

Three taps. One great note.

No editors, no typing, no friction. Just speak — and Jot It AI does the rest.

🎙

Tap the mic and speak naturally. Notes, meetings, ideas, reflections — anything goes. Jot captures it all.

02

Features

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Built for people who think out loud. Jot It AI turns raw voice into structured, shareable notes.

🔑

Bring Your Own Key

Connect your own API key — OpenAI, Claude, or Grok. No markup, no black box pricing. You pay the provider directly — typically pennies per note.

🧠

AI-Structured Notes

Choose Light (clean transcript), Medium (organized), or Full (fully rewritten). AI generates summaries, sections, and action items.

✍️

6 Writing Styles

Simple & Clear, Meeting Notes, Journal Entry, Bullet Points, Email Draft, Blog Post. Your note, your voice, your format.

📂

Smart Folders

Organize notes into folders — Business, Journal, Work, Content Ideas. Auto-tagging keeps everything tidy without effort.

🔗

Seamless Export

Send notes to Notion, export as Markdown, or copy to clipboard. Append new recordings to existing notes anytime.

🌍

Multi-language

Transcribe in your language, structure in your preferred output format. English, Spanish, French, and more supported.

✅

Smart Task Extraction

AI automatically detects action items in your notes and adds them to a built-in todo list. Check off tasks as you complete them — no manual entry needed.
